Origami Instructions and Diagrams


In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin. The goal is to transform a flat square sheet of paper into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques.

Interesting facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • Chameleons live in warm varied habitats from rainforests through to deserts.
  • There are around 160 species of chameleon.

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bear

  • The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.

Interesting facts about Panda

  1. Panda have great camouflage for their environment.

  3. Pandas occasionally eat something other than bamboo.

Interesting facts about Frog

  • A group of frogs is called an army.

Interesting facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Cicada's short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male Cicada chorus.
